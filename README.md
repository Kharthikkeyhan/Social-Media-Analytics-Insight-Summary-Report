# Social-Media-Analytics-Insight-Summary-Report
🎯 Project Objectives
Clean, preprocess, and structure the social media dataset by handling missing values, removing duplicates, correcting data types, and performing feature engineering to prepare the data for analysis.
Analyze the performance of different post formats (Image, Video, Reel, and Text) using key engagement metrics such as likes, comments, shares, impressions, watch time, engagement rate, and engagement score.
Compare content performance across categories, countries, and audience segments to identify the highest and lowest performing content and regional engagement patterns.
Examine user behavior by analyzing demographic factors such as age groups, account verification status, device types, and posting times to understand their impact on audience engagement.
Evaluate the influence of audience sentiment (Positive, Neutral, and Negative) on content reach, engagement, and overall social media performance.
Perform comprehensive exploratory data analysis (EDA), including univariate, bivariate, and multivariate analysis, to discover trends, correlations, patterns, and outliers within the dataset.
Generate meaningful insights and business recommendations that support data-driven decision-making for improving content strategy, audience targeting, posting schedules, and overall social media marketing effectiveness.
✨ Key Highlights
📊 Analyzed 5,000 social media posts from 10 countries covering the period 2022–2023.
🧹 Performed data cleaning, preprocessing, and feature engineering to improve data quality and analytical accuracy.
📈 Conducted Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) using descriptive statistics and interactive visualizations.
🎥 Evaluated the performance of Image, Video, Reel, and Text content formats using multiple engagement metrics.
🌍 Compared engagement trends across countries, content categories, age groups, device types, and verified vs. non-verified accounts.
😊 Performed sentiment analysis to assess the impact of Positive, Neutral, and Negative content on audience engagement.
⏰ Identified the most effective posting times for maximizing impressions and engagement.
📉 Applied statistical analysis including measures of central tendency, dispersion, skewness, kurtosis, and correlation analysis.
📊 Created univariate, bivariate, and multivariate visualizations such as histograms, box plots, scatter plots, correlation heatmaps, pair plots, grouped analyses, and pivot tables.
💡 Delivered actionable business insights and recommendations to optimize content strategy, audience engagement, and social media marketing performance
