# Social-Media-Analytics-Insight-Summary-Report
## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Clean, preprocess, and transform the social media dataset by handling missing values, removing duplicate records, correcting data types, and performing feature engineering to prepare the data for analysis.
- Analyze the performance of different post formats (**Image, Video, Reel, and Text**) using engagement metrics such as **Likes, Comments, Shares, Impressions, Watch Time, Engagement Rate,** and **Engagement Score**.
- Compare engagement across different **content categories, countries, and audience segments** to identify high-performing content and regional engagement patterns.
- Examine user behavior by analyzing **age groups, account verification status, device types,** and **posting schedules** to understand their impact on audience engagement.
- Evaluate the influence of **Positive, Neutral,** and **Negative** sentiment on content reach, impressions, engagement, and overall social media performance.
- Perform comprehensive **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** through **Univariate, Bivariate, and Multivariate Analysis** to identify trends, relationships, correlations, and outliers.
- Generate meaningful insights and business recommendations to support **data-driven content strategy, audience targeting, posting optimization,** and **social media marketing decisions**.

---

## ✨ Key Highlights

- 📊 **Analyzed 5,000 social media posts** collected from **10 countries** covering the period **2022–2023**.
- 🧹 Performed **Data Cleaning, Data Transformation,** and **Feature Engineering** to improve data quality and analysis.
- 📈 Conducted **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** using descriptive statistics and data visualization techniques.
- 🎥 Evaluated the performance of **Image, Video, Reel,** and **Text** content formats using multiple engagement metrics.
- 🌍 Compared audience engagement across **Countries, Content Categories, Age Groups, Device Types,** and **Verified vs. Non-Verified Accounts**.
- 😊 Analyzed **Audience Sentiment (Positive, Neutral, and Negative)** to understand its impact on engagement and reach.
- ⏰ Identified the **optimal posting time** for maximizing impressions and audience interaction.
- 📉 Applied **Statistical Analysis** including **Measures of Central Tendency, Dispersion, Skewness, Kurtosis,** and **Correlation Analysis**.
- 📊 Created **Univariate, Bivariate, and Multivariate Visualizations** using **Histograms, Box Plots, Scatter Plots, Correlation Heatmaps, Pair Plots, Grouped Analysis,** and **Pivot Tables**.
- 💡 Generated **actionable insights and strategic recommendations** to optimize content performance, audience engagement, and social media marketing effectiveness.
